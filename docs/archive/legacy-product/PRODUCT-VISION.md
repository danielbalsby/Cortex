# Product Vision

Cortex exists to give clinicians more time with patients by reducing
administrative duplication and cognitive friction.

The first product milestone is deliberately narrow:

> Build the most intuitive digital workflow for a knee-pain consultation in
> Danish general practice.

Success is not measured by the number of features. It is measured by whether a
clinician voluntarily opens Cortex again for the next relevant patient.
