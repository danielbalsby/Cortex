# Clinical Principles

- Outputs are drafts and require clinician review.
- Cortex may remind, suggest and identify missing information.
- Cortex must not present a diagnosis as established automatically.
- Acute clinical concerns must be visible and explainable.
- Normal findings should not create long, low-value journal text.
- Positive and decision-relevant negative findings should be prioritised.
- Referral requirements must be source-based, versioned and region-aware.
