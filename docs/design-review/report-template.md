## Findings by severity

For each finding: ID, severity, observation, inference, scenario, reproduction steps, expected/observed result, evidence, effect, confidence, recommendation, owner, and disposition.

## Safety blockers

List blockers and required disposition, or state “None identified in the evaluated scope.”

## Strengths to preserve

List evidenced qualities that must not regress.

## Reviewer conflicts

Record disagreement, evidence on each side, and Chair disposition; do not invent consensus.

## Consolidated decision

Select and justify one outcome from [scoring and decisions](scoring.md). State blockers and gate consequences explicitly.
