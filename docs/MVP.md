# MVP

## User

A general practitioner or trainee working in Danish general practice.

## Primary workflow

A patient presents with knee pain.

## Required outcomes

- Short clinical journal
- Relevant clinical review
- X-ray referral draft when selected
- Physiotherapy referral draft when selected
- Minimal duplicate entry
- Minimal editing after copying

## Pilot exit criteria

- A new clinician understands the interface without a manual
- Routine documentation takes less than 90 seconds after examination
- Generated notes require minimal editing
- Clinical review is noticed without disrupting workflow
- The clinician wants to use Cortex again
